# Emberglass: Covenant of the Skywell

## Game Design Document

### Setting
The world is suspended around a broken celestial well called the Skywell. Cities survive on floating glass islands. Emberglass crystals preserve memories, but the memories are starting to lie.

### Party
- **Nara**: Cartographer, balanced attacker, map-based skills
- **Kael**: Disgraced shrine-guard, tank/counter, ember blade
- **Io**: Runaway machine-oracle, support/status/arcane tech

### Plot
A young cartographer, a disgraced shrine-guard, and a runaway machine-oracle uncover that the world's map has been edited by a sleeping god beneath the clouds.

### Regions
1. **Luma Quay** — Hub town on floating glass island
2. **Verdant Archive** — Forest ruins, overgrown library
3. **Mirrordrift** — Desert glass field, mirrored dunes
4. **Thunderveil** — Storm shrine, lightning temple
5. **Skywell Core** — Final dungeon, broken celestial well

### Structure
- Prologue → 3 regions → 3 dungeons → 4 bosses → final dungeon → ending
- 16 enemy types, 4 bosses with phases
- 8+ optional secrets
- Target: 45-90 minutes

### Combat
- Turn-based, party of 3
- Systems: basic attack, skills, defend, items, escape, elemental weaknesses, stagger meter, Resonance abilities, status effects, turn timeline
- Juice: hit pause, screen shake, particles, damage numbers, flash frames

### Progression
- XP, levels, stats, skill unlocks
- Equipment: weapon, charm, relic
- Items: potions, ethers, revive, status cures
- Ember Shards for unique ability upgrades

### Palette
- Ember orange, deep teal, storm violet, moonlit blue, glass gold

### Controls
- Keyboard: Arrow/WASD + Enter/Space + Esc
- Mouse/Touch: Click/Tap
- Gamepad: D-pad + A/B/X/Y
